A Rubber Duck or Rubber Ducky is a toy shaped like a very simplistic Duck with a flat bottom. Originally created to sooth fears of water and improve developmental skills in children during water play the Rubber Duck has become so much more over time.

Rubber ducks have even been featured in books

"One is never alone with a rubber duck."-Douglas Adams

The many uses of a Rubber Duck:

  • Learn about buoyancy
  • Take a bath
  • Release stress
  • Have a puppet show
  • Talk to the Duck to problem solve
